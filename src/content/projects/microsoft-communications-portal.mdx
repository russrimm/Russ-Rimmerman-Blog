Stay ahead of change without living in a dozen admin centers. The Microsoft
Communications Portal aggregates service health, roadmap items, and Message
Center posts across Microsoft 365 and Azure into a single pane of glass — so IT
teams can track what's changing, plan for it, and communicate clearly.
