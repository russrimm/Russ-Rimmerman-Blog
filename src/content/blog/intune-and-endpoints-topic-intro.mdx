If you landed here, you found the corner of the blog dedicated to **Intune &
Endpoints** — managing and securing devices for the modern workplace.

Consider this the welcome mat. There aren't a pile of deep-dive posts here yet,
but this is where they'll live as I publish them. I wanted to put a stake in the
ground so the topic hub has a home and you know what to expect.

## What this topic will cover

Endpoint management is where security policy meets real people and their
laptops. The posts I'm planning here come from years in the field, including:

- **Device configuration and compliance** — baselines that secure devices
  without generating a support queue.
- **Endpoint security and hardening** — the settings that actually reduce risk
  versus the ones that just look good in a report.
- **Modern workplace at scale** — managing a fleet without treating every device
  as a one-off.

## Why start here

Every topic on this blog gets a short introductory post like this one so the hub
has a home from day one. More to come — if there's a specific Intune or endpoint
scenario you'd like me to tackle first, the [contact page](/contact) is the
fastest way to reach me.
